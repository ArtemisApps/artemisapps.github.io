Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the mobile applications and digital products developed by Artemis Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use any of our mobile applications, tools, or services (collectively, the “Services”).


About Artemis Lab

Artemis Lab is an independent mobile software developer. We are not affiliated, associated, authorized, endorsed by, or officially connected with any third-party companies or platforms mentioned in our Services.


Information We Collect

0. No Collection of Your Personal Content
We do not collect, upload, store, share, analyze, or transmit any personal content from your device—including photos, videos, messages, notifications, media files, or documents—to Artemis Lab servers or any third-party servers. All content processing required for app features (such as photo analysis, file scanning, message recovery, or device storage optimization) takes place entirely on your device. Your personal content never leaves your phone and is never accessible to us or to any external party.

1. Advertising Identifiers:
We may access your device’s advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID, Apple IDFA) to provide personalized ads, frequency capping, fraud detection, and performance analytics. These identifiers do not reveal your name, files, photos, messages, or direct personal identity.

2. Device & Technical Log Data:
We automatically collect non-personal technical information to ensure stable, secure, and optimized app performance. This may include:

  • Error logs and crash diagnostics
  • Performance metrics

3. Usage Analytics:
We may collect anonymized usage metrics (e.g., which features are used) to improve product functionality. No personal content such as photos, videos, messages, files, or private media is ever uploaded or accessed by us.

4. In-App Permissions:
If the Services require access to features like camera, photo library, notifications, or storage, such permissions are used strictly for core app functionality. All processing is performed locally on your device and not transmitted to any server.


How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Deliver personalized and contextual advertising
  • Analyze user behavior and improve features
  • Provide crash diagnostics and performance insights
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and system misuse

We do not sell your personal information.

We also do not share any personal information or personal content—such as your photos, videos, messages, documents, or any files stored on your device—with Artemis Lab servers or with any third-party companies. All sensitive processing performed by the Services occurs entirely on your device and never leaves your phone.

GDPR (European Union) Compliance

If you reside in the EU/EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccuracies
  • Right to delete your personal data (“Right to be Forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Legal bases for processing include legitimate interests, consent, and contractual necessity.


CCPA / CPRA (California) Compliance

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) provides you rights to:

  • Request disclosure of the categories of data collected
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of data “sharing” for advertising purposes
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not “sell” personal data as defined under the CCPA.


LGPD (Brazil) Compliance

For users in Brazil, we comply with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD). You have rights to access, correct, delete, anonymize, or object to processing of your personal data. Data is processed based on consent, legitimate interest, or contractual necessity.


Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we delete the data immediately.
